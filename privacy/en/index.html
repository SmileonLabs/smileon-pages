Privacy Policy

Smileon ("Company") is committed to protecting the personal information of users of Velo ("Service") in accordance with applicable privacy laws, including the Korean Personal Information Protection Act. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Photos & Videos: Your photos and videos are accessed solely for compression purposes and are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded to or stored on our servers. All compression operations are performed 100% offline on the user's device.

3. Retention and Disposal of Personal Information

The Company promptly destroys personal information once the purpose of collection and use has been fulfilled.

  • Account deletion: Immediately destroyed
  • Purchase history: Retained for 5 years in accordance with the Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, then destroyed
  • Crash logs: Automatically deleted 90 days after collection

4. Sharing Personal Information with Third Parties

The Company does not share users' personal information with third parties as a general principle. Exceptions apply only in the following cases:

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by applicable laws or regulations

6. Users' Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Right to access: You may request to view the personal information we have collected.
  • Right to correction: You may request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Right to deletion (account deletion): You may delete your account via Settings > Delete Account in the app, which will immediately destroy all personal information.
  • Right to suspend processing: You may request suspension of personal information processing.

These rights may be exercised through in-app features or by contacting us via the email address below.

7. Measures to Ensure Security of Personal Information

  • HTTPS (TLS) encryption applied to all data transmissions
  • No user passwords stored (OAuth-based social login used)
  • Photos and videos are never transmitted to servers; processed only on the device
  • Principle of least privilege applied to access controls

8. Automatic Data Collection and Opt-Out

The Service does not use cookies. App crash logs and performance data are automatically collected to ensure service stability and are processed in a form that does not identify specific individuals.

9. Personal Information of Children Under 14

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 14, and we do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 14. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 14, we will promptly delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated in accordance with changes in applicable laws or the Service. Any changes will be communicated in advance through in-app notifications or email.
